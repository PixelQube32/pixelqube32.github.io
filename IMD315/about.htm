Welcome to Socimedia. This blog is to review any media I look at followed by a short analysis of the media piece using Sociology. Here I will review movies, tv shows, any form of media I come into contact with and provide a Sociological feedback. Society affects media and in turn media affects us. Once we have an understanding of how the world works we can begin to change it for the better. Media and society are intertwined deeply. Popular TV and Movies have a great influence on us and our tastes changes as well as influence what is acceptable to be viewed and made for the future.
